Discover Lavina

We believe that tea enriches life and the bond between people. Just as we believe that sincerity and honesty adds a lot to our relationships. Lavina tea is a rich indulgence of taste and quality.

Our tea leaves are carefully chosen to bring you a sensuous taste that makes drinking tea an occasion to join loved ones together, creating a candid environment full of human warmness, genuine communication and friendly conversations.

Knot Technology

Our tea bags are put together using zero attachment metals or substances. The tea bags are knotted by a highly sophisticated machine, to create a product enriching in taste and healthy to drink.

Sealed for Freshness

Providing fresh, enriching tea for your drinking pleasure goes beyond the careful selection of tea leaves. We manufacture a product that preserves its freshness naturally through sealing technology, bringing you the sensuous taste of our tea leaves to your convenience.

Eco-friendly Filter Paper

To ensure that Lavina teas are infused into your cup in the best way possible, we produced special branded filter paper purely out of natural fibers, to achieve giving you the most rich taste and the environment maximum consideration.
